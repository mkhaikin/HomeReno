Interior painting

A fresh coat of paint
a simple move yet a determining Factor

Let's get rolling

Choosing the colour paint for one room could be quite a challenge. Choosing the colour scheme for your new home now that's probably excruciating. But it sure doesn't have to be. We can help you understand the process better by guiding you across the colour picker. We can help you pick out colours and tones that will resonate better across your entire living space.

Interior wall paint

when first testing out some of the paint samples that you have selected. We recommend doing so in the daylight. You will get a better feel for the colour this way. If the room does not have direct sunlight, it's important to paint a few samples on adjacent walls to see if the lighting affects the shade. However, proper wall preparation could be the game-changer. We will take care of all necessary before and after work so you start enjoying your new walls.

Trim and molding painting

Painting and refreshing the trim is a great way to liven any room and give it that clean feeling. Professionally installed trim is an absolute joy to the eyes, which is why a room with baseboards coated with the right amount of shine can create a peaceful and aesthetically pleasing look. Not to mention the ease of cleaning. We have lots of simple tricks and hacks that can help you pretty up a room from the trim to crown moulding and everything in between. Every paint job starts with taping and ends with a spotless look. It's our priority to make your home look and feel great top to bottom.

Miscellaneous & outdoor painting

There are lots of items on the list to paint when moving into a new home or moving out of an existing one. Your list may include items such as painting or restaining staircases, pickets, ledges and many more decorations. Choosing the right finish is important to the consistency and overall look of your home. We always chose a double coat. However, in some cases where the desired colour is much different than the original one we say third time is the charm. With the overall tone of the room, we can help you add some interesting feel to your painting project.
