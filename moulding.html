Trim and molding

Give your rooms
A more dramatic and luxurious look

A little goes a long way

We love working with various trims and mouldings. We find this little feature can go a long way. Adding trim can create a more sophisticated look to any room. Clean white baseboards are just eye-popping and gorgeous and crown mouldings are a simple way to make your bedroom lookmore luxurious. While designing custom panels can give youjust the style you were looking for.

Baseboards and Trim

We have been installing baseboards and trim for years and we've been doing so with precision. Workling with corners and tight spaces is our specialty. We enjoy the challenge of working with angles to create the perfect look. Our baseboards come out truly eye-popping. Attention to deal is very important in these situation because some floors are not all perfectly levelled.

Trim and molding

Adding a middle tier to a wall creates a much fuller and sophisticated room. It's important not to overcrowd the wall but rather to enhance it. Panels and various trim are a great way to create separation between the bottom and top portions of the wall. The right design or combination is something we are more than happy to assist you with.

Crown Moulding

The cherry on top. Crown moulding adds elegance to a room. It demands your attention and it sure deserves it. Adding crowing moulding to any room is a guaranteed way to upscale your look without breaking the bank. Designs come in many different shapes and sizes, Our team can help you narrow the look and lock in your choice.
