Choosing between hardwood and laminate.

This question has been asked since the dawn of time. Every homeowner always wanted to know which choice to make between going with the cheaper laminate or longlasting hardwood. perhaps engineered, a combination of the two. When reflooring it's important to understand the pros and cons.

Laminate

Laminate installation is the cheaper, simpler alternative to re-flooring your home. Installing laminate can be turn out to be a quick job especially with some of the new click technology available with an increasing number of laminate manufacturing companies. Laminate is able to handle the test of time but doens't the rich wooden look that hard wood has. It is though a very budget friendly approach.

Hardwood

Hardwood flooring creates a more inviting and warmer look. The natural feel of some of the hand-scraped hardwood floors is simply a joy to walk on barefoot. Hardwood though more costly, is a great option when deciding to remodel long-term. Hardwood floors come in a range of colours and widths as well as textures and finishes. Hardwood can be installed in different patterns and angles complementing any room.

Engineered

Another great way to decorate your kitchen is with backsplash. Backsplash is the area located behind the sink or stove. It's the area that protects the wall from splashing. Different designs and materials can be used to really enhance the over-all look.
