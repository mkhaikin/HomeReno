Tiling

Beautiful. Water resistant. Durable.

Let's get tiling

Tiling is one of our specialties. We have many years of experience laying tiles just about everywhere. Tiles are resistant to water and most stains, which makes them the perfect choice in so many places. They are durable and great for wet areas like bathrooms, kitchens and laundry rooms. Tiles are easy to clean and maintain. Tiles are made from various materials some of the commonly known ones are ceramic, glass, marble and the list goes on.

Ceramic Tiles

Ceramic tiles are one of the most common types of tiles found throughout the home. Ceramic tiles come in 3 types. Glazed, unglazed and porcelain. Each comes in a large array of shapes and sizes that can be arranged together to form desgin pattern. Today, many ceramic tiles look just like a natural stone at a fracture of the price. They area great for busy kitchens and are easy to clean. We can help you choose the right tile to create the right effect according to your room size.

Choosing the tiles

When starting on a bathroom tiling project, choosing the right tiles can be overwhelming. It's important to understand the purpose of the tile you wish to purchase and where it can be applied. A porcelain tile is one of the hardest and densest tiles available on the market. Incorporating several tiles into a bathroom can create a beautiful lasting design. Porcelain tiles are non-porous and can resist scratches. They are also known to withstand temperature extremes.

Tile design

When tiling a wall or a floor, a little tile design can add character and even make a room appear bigger. Choosing the right tile combination is a matter of appearance and functionality. Incorporating natural stones to a high traffic area can greatly reduce cracks and future maintenance problems. We can show and recommend several grid patterns that can add a style and work with your tiling project.
