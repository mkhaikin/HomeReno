About Us

Alex Home Renovations is a full-service home design and installation company. Our focus is on achieving simplicity and elegance throughout your living space. We specialize in home and condo renovation and have a rapidly growing list of happy customers. In today's competitive market it's important to understand and fully meet the client's requirements all while staying within budget. Our years of experience will aid you during the entire renovation process allowing you to make the transitions and get back to living.

Our English, Russian and Hebrew speaking team will be happy to answer all your questions regarding your next upcoming reno idea. We will be happy to assist and guide you every step of the way into your newly transformed living space. We will help you plan and organize so you can feel comfortable knowing what and how long it will take to complete your renovation. We will take a load off you so you don't have to worry about every detail. We have completed many projects and our customers are happy to vouch for us. We will make sure your home is not a never-ending construction site by helping you schedule the workload. We are focused on one project at a time so you know our attention is on completing the task.

We are eager to get to know and earn your business. We will be happy to provide a FREE consultation on your project and help you explore your possibilities. We appreciate you visiting us and would like to hear from you. You can reach us by email or by phone.
