Get ready, set flush...

It's often a bit of trouble to get yourself planned and organized even for a simple bathroom renovation. It's not uncommon to find yourself lost not knowing where to start or better yet, to have way too many ideas and not enough courage to commit to just one. It's important to focus on a few key aspects when planning to renovate a bathroom. We would like to share a few tips with you that can help you hit the target.

Bath / Shower

It's important to start the planning process by understanding the space that you are working with. The first decision you should be making is whether you'll be installing a bathtub or a shower. It's important to consider the pros and cons but most importantly the purpose of your bathroom. A master bathroom will have a differnt look and purpose than a family bathroom.

Sink

A good way to set the tone of a newly renovated bathroom is to pick out a great vanity. The right vanity can make your bathroom space look modern and elegent while still meeting all you're requirments. Whether you choose a light or darker bathroom the sink is what brings it all together. An important thing to keep in mind is the need for storage space. A neat vanity can help you stay stylish and organized at the same time.

Toilet

Bathroom renos can be as simple as changing and updating your current toilet. Old toilets are not as water efficient as the current toilets available on the market. When faced with renovating a bathroom that includes that main toilet in the living space make sure to consider a water conserving toilet to help you save and reduce water consumption. Stop flashing your money down and start saving long term.
